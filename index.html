02 · The Mechanism

Capture. Disinfect.
Then Walk Away.

Sports Skunk works on two fronts. Zinc ricinoleate — derived from the castor bean plant — forms a stable chemical bond with odor molecules, making them physically undetectable. The smell isn't covered. It's captured.

An alcohol base works alongside it as a Health Canada-licensed antibacterial — killing the odor-causing bacteria that re-grow inside damp gear between uses. Capture the smell that's there. Stop the smell that's coming.

No synthetic fragrance. No "Fresh Rain" cover-up. Just a light, natural orange-mint from real essential oils — there to scent the room as you spray, not to mask what's underneath.
